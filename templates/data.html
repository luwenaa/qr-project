data policies and information

purpose

the purpose of this tool is to process text input locally, and generate a qr code. this tool follows guidelines to ensure data integrity.

safety warning

  • do not enter sensitive personal data.
  • do not share qr codes containing private information.
  • ensure inputs comply with university data protection policies.
  • qr outputs are not encrypted.

data storage

this tool processes data locally in your browser.

no text is uploaded, stored, logged, or transmitted.

your input is cleared automatically when you close the page.

keeping safe

  • store your qr images securely.
  • avoid posting qr codes publicly unless safe.
  • delete old qr files when no longer needed.
  • use this tool only for appropriate purposes.
