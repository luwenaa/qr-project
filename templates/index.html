the qr space

a simple secure tool to generate your text into a scannable qr code.

data protection & user consent

please read our data policies and information!

purpose

the purpose of this tool is to process text input locally, and generate a qr code. this tool follows guidelines to ensure data integrity.

safety warning

this tool processes only the text you provide.

  • do not enter sensitive personal data.
  • do not share qr codes containing private information.
  • ensure inputs complies with university data protection policies
  • qr outputs are not encrypted, anyone who scans them can read the data.

data storage

this tool processes data locally in your browser.
⟶ no text is uploaded, stored, logged, or transmitted to any server.
⟶ once you close the page, your input is automatically cleared.

keeping safe

  • keep your generated qr images stored securely on your device.
  • avoid posting qr codes publicly unless the encoded text is safe for anyone to read.
  • delete older qr files if they are no longer needed.
  • only use this tool for appropriate academic or personal purposes.
