how to generate a qr code

follow this quick guide to turn your text into a scannable qr code.

step 1

open the generator

go to qr code. you’ll see a simple text box where you can type anything you want to convert.

⟶ note: a data information pop-up may appear, please read thoroughly , tick 'i agree' and confirm button before proceeding.

step 2

input your text

add the text you want to encode! this could be:

  1. a short message
  2. a to-do list
  3. a snippet of information
  4. a study note
  5. anything safe to be shared

⟶ note: do not enter personal or sensitive data! — qr codes are not encrypted.

step 3

click “generate qr”

once you press the button, your qr code will appear instantly — everything stays in your browser.

step 4

wait for your qr to generate

(this should only take a moment!)

step 5

download your qr

below the qr code, choose your preferred download format and save it.

step 6

test your qr code

scan the qr with your phone to make sure everything works correctly.

⟢ taa-daa! now you’ve made your own scannable qr code!
